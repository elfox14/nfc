How do I change the logo or name position?

Simply press and hold the element you want to move and drag it to the new position.

QR Code doesn't appear, what should I do?

Ensure a source is selected from 'QR Code' section. Recommended is 'Card Link', then click 'Generate/Update QR Code'.

How do I save the design as an image?

From the top preview bar, click 'Preview' and select 'Front Image' or 'Back Image'.
