How do I move the logo or name?

Simply click and hold the element you want to move (such as the logo or text box) and drag it to the new position on the card.

The QR code isn't showing, what do I do?

Make sure to select a source for the QR code in the "QR Code" section. The recommended option is "Card Link", then click the "Generate/Update QR Code" button.

How do I save the design as an image?

From the top toolbar, click the "Download" button and select "Front Image" or "Back Image".
