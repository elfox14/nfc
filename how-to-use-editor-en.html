Editor Overview

The MC PRIME editor is a free and powerful tool for designing smart digital business cards (NFC). It features an easy-to-use interface that allows you to create a fully professional card without any technical experience.

Editor Interface Components

Left Panel

Control layout, backgrounds, and ready-made designs

Preview Area

Live preview with ability to flip between front and back

Right Panel

Control elements: logo, photo, info, social links

Toolbar

Save, share, download, and undo buttons

Tip

Your design is automatically saved while you work. You can see the save status in the top toolbar.

Choosing Layout

The editor provides three basic layouts for your card:

  • Classic: Traditional business card layout with balanced element distribution
  • Modern: Contemporary design with vertical content division
  • Vertical: Perfect for extensive content with vertical element arrangement

Ready-made Designs

You can start from one of the ready-made designs and modify it according to your needs. Click on any design in the design gallery and it will be applied instantly.

Smart Suggestion

Use the "Suggest Smart Design" button to get a random color-coordinated design!

Backgrounds & Colors

Full control over the background of each side of the card:

Front Side

  • Choose two colors to create a beautiful gradient
  • Or upload an image to use as background
  • Control color overlay transparency

Back Side

  • Same customization options available for the back
  • You can use different colors for each side

Image Tip

For best results, use images with dimensions 1050×600 pixels. You can drag and drop images directly onto the upload area.

Adding Logo

Add your company logo or brand to the card:

  • Click on the "Logo" section in the right panel
  • Upload your logo image (automatically cropped)
  • Control logo size using the slider
  • Choose logo alignment (left, center, right)
  • Add background to logo if needed

Advanced Settings

  • Opacity: Control logo transparency
  • Shadow: Add shadow to make logo stand out
  • Position: Choose to display on front or back
  • Fine Movement: Use arrows to move logo by pixel

Profile Photo

Add your personal photo to give your card a personal touch:

  • Upload your photo or enter image URL
  • Choose photo shape (circular or square)
  • Control photo size and border
  • Add border color and control width
  • Enable shadow to highlight the photo

Note

It's recommended to use a high-quality photo with a clear background. The image will be automatically cropped on upload.

Personal Information

Enter your name and job title:

Full Name

  • Write your name in Arabic or English
  • Control font size and color
  • Choose font type (Tajawal, Cairo, Lalezar)
  • Set name position (front or back)

Job Title / Business

  • Write your position or field of work
  • Same formatting options available
  • You can write multiple lines

Bilingual Support

You can enter name and job title in both Arabic and English. Use the language toggle button in the toolbar to switch between languages.

Phone Numbers

Add phone numbers for direct contact:

  • Click "Add Phone Number" to add a new number
  • Enter number with country code (e.g., +1234567890)
  • Choose display method: as buttons or as text
  • You can add multiple numbers

Button Customization

  • Control button color and text color
  • Adjust font size and padding
  • Choose font type

Interactive Buttons

When viewing the card on a phone, recipients can tap the button to call directly!

QR Code

Add a QR code for quick access to your card:

QR Source Options

  • Card Link (Recommended): Creates QR for your card's share link
  • Contact Data (vCard): Contains your info for direct contact saving
  • Use Link: Enter any link you want
  • Upload Image: Use your own ready-made QR code

Appearance Customization

  • Change code color and background color
  • Choose dot shape (squares, dots, rounded)
  • Choose corner shape
  • Include your logo in the center of the code

Contact & Social Links

Add all ways to contact you:

Fixed Data

  • Email: For email contact
  • Website: Your website or portfolio link
  • WhatsApp: For direct WhatsApp contact
  • Facebook: Your Facebook page link
  • Instagram: Your Instagram account
  • Twitter/X: Your Twitter account
  • LinkedIn: Your professional profile
  • TikTok: Your TikTok account
  • YouTube: Your YouTube channel
  • Snapchat: Your Snapchat account
  • Location: Google Maps link to your location

Custom Links

You can add additional links with appropriate icons.

Display Settings

  • Display as buttons with icons
  • Or display as plain text
  • Control button colors and sizes
  • Choose position for each link (front or back)

Save & Share

Save Design

  • Click "Save Design" button in the toolbar
  • Your design will be saved to the cloud
  • You'll get a unique share link for your card
  • You can return to edit the design anytime

Download Options

  • PNG Image: High-quality image of front or back
  • PDF File: For professional printing
  • VCF Contact: To save data to phone
  • QR Code: Download QR code as image

Sharing

  • Click "Share" button to get card link
  • Share link via any messaging app
  • Or copy link to paste anywhere

Save to Gallery

Use "Save to Gallery" button to save your design locally and access it from the gallery page.
