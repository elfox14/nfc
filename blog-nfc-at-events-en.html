5 Innovative Ways to Use NFC Cards at Events & Conferences

1. Instant Conversion to Subscriber

Configure your smart card to direct users straight to your newsletter subscription page or LinkedIn profile with a single tap.

2. Showcase Interactive Portfolio

Set your card to open directly to your best project or a video showcasing your skills, leaving a stronger impact than just swapping names.

3. Share Exclusive Event Offers

Create a landing page with a special discount or free consultation available only to those you meet at the event to encourage further engagement.

4. Lead Generation

Link your card to a simple contact form so interested clients can register their details instantly for quotes or more information.

5. Digital Menu or Booking Link

For restaurants or service businesses, the card can direct straight to a digital menu or booking page, providing a seamless modern experience.
