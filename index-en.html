Frequently Asked Questions

What is the real value of this card?

It's more than just a card ‚Äî it's a modern marketing tool. It saves you printing costs, is eco-friendly, and allows you to update your information instantly, ensuring your details are always accurate for your clients.

How is my data and privacy secured?

We take privacy seriously. Your data is used only to display your card and we do not share or sell it to any third party. You can view our full privacy policy for more details.

Do you offer technical support?

Absolutely. We provide full support to help you through any step. If you encounter any issues while designing your card or have any questions, you can contact us directly through our contact page.

How do I order a physical NFC card?

After designing and saving your card, you can contact us through the "Contact Us" page to order a physical NFC card. We will program the card with your digital card link and ship it to you.

Which phones are compatible with NFC cards?

Most modern smartphones support NFC, including: iPhone 7 and newer (iOS 11+), and all NFC-enabled Android phones (most phones since 2015). You can check your phone settings to confirm.

Does the card work with iPhone?

Yes! iPhone 7 and newer support reading NFC cards. For iPhones, just bring the phone close to the card and a notification will appear to open your card page. You can also always scan the QR code as an alternative.

How long does shipping take?

We ship cards within 3-5 business days within Egypt, and 7-14 business days for other Arab countries. We'll send you a tracking link once shipped.
